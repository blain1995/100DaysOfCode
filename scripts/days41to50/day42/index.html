My Skills.

Bioinformatics analysis

During my PhD I have been learning Bash, R and Python programming in order to carry out bioinformatic analysis of NGS datasets of paediatric lymphoma patients.

My project includes somatic variant identification from whole exome sequencing data, structural variant identification of whole genome sequencing data, copy number aberration analysis of SNP arrays and RNA-sequencing.

Lab based analysis

I am now confident in nucleic acid extraction techniques, tapestation analysis, NGS library preparation and NanoString nCounter expression analysis.
